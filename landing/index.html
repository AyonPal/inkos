FAQ

Questions? We've got answers.

Do clients need to download an app?

No. Booking, deposit payment, and consent signing all happen in a mobile web browser. No app install, no account creation for the client.

Can I customize the consent form?

Yes. You provide the questions, we provide the form builder. Your consent form, your language. Signed forms are stored in the client's booking record and can be downloaded as PDFs.

How does Stripe deposit payout work?

Deposits go through your own Stripe account. We don't hold your money. Standard Stripe payout timelines apply — usually 2 business days. You keep everything minus Stripe's fee.

Can I customize the aftercare messages?

Absolutely. Default messages are ready to go on day 1, but you can edit them per session type. Flash tattoo vs. sleeve will have different aftercare needs.

Is there a free trial?

Yes — 14 days free, no credit card required. Take it for a full spin before deciding. If it doesn't replace your DM chaos, just cancel.
